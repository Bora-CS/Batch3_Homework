Göztepe Spor Kulübü also known as Göztepe, is a Turkish sports club located in the Güzelyalı neighborhood of İzmir, Turkey. The "SK" refers to sports club, as besides football the club also has departments in fencing, triathlon, handball, sailing, swimming, and windsurfing.
